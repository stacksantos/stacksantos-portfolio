THE PROCESS
How it works
  1. We schedule a chat to talk about your needs and budget for the project.
  2. I'll create the proposal and contract then send it to you via email.
  3. You'll review everything and sign only when you are comfortable with all of the terms.
  4. LETS GET TO WORK!
  5. I'll stay in touch with any updates on the project.
  6. When finished, we'll review and go over any concerns you have.
  7. If everything looks A-Ok, you'll sumbit the payment and I'll deliver the project!
